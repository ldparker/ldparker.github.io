Surfing

Cycling

From May-mid June of 2016, I cycled from Vancouver, British Columbia down to the border of Mexico near San Diego. This journey totalled 3,100km and took 33 days of cycling. I biked from Vancouver to the border of California and Oregon with my friend Tanner, and cycled the remaining distance alone. I camped along the way, carrying everything I needed for the 5 week venture on my bicycle.
