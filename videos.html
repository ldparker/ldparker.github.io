Lake Louise Birthday Edit

Filmed by myself and friends on my 18th birthday at Lake Louise, Alberta.

My brother Lawson, my friends Jackson and Patrick, and myself played hooky for the day, and went skiing instead at Lake Louise Ski Resort for my 18th birthday. We decided to take a camera and document the day, and this is what we came up with. We all took turns filming, and I did the editing.
